    About BUGSB!TE

    BUGSB!TE is an alternative indie rock band that creates songs focusing on topics such as individuality, personal experiences and social issues through the themes of nature. The band formed 5 years ago in June 2020. BUGSB!TE consists of 5 members that go by the stage names Mantis, Dragon, Hopper, Lady and Honey.

    BUGSB!TE most recent album "Make It Ours" is out now! Give it a listen and buy our merch to show your support!

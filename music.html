    About "Make it Ours"

    “Make It Ours”by BUGSB!TE was released on December 2nd 2025. There are 10 tracks total. The album takes us through a story of resilience of victims of corrupted power. The inspiration for “Make It Ours” came from each member's personal experience and how they were able to push through the hardships of life.

    "We hope our songs can help provide the little boost of strength people need to get past the obstacles they are challenged with.”

    - Mantis, BUGSB!TE’s main vocalist.
